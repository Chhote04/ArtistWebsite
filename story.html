About me :- My art journey started when I was 5-6 years old and used to see my elder brother's drawing and I got inspired to draw from that. I always used to make elephant , horses from my mother because I did not know how to draw. Then I also tried to draw and started drawing well while doing it. I earned a lot of name in my school life and participated in many art competitions.When I came to class 11 in the year 2017, seeing my interest in art, one day my teacher Dixit maam told me about fine art and since then I thought of becoming a professional artist In the year 2019.I passed class 12 from commerce and took 1 year drop. During the year drop I met my new friend Riyaz and he taught me a lot and gave informations about new things in art and I also taught him new things and since then till today we both work together. In the year 2020 i enrolled in BFA from govt institute of fine arts collage ,gwalior (MP). Now I'm student of painting but i am interested in all type of arts.
